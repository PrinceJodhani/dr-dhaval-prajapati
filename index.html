ECT

Electroconvulsive Therapy (ECT) available for treating patients with mania, depression, and schizophrenia.

EEG

EEG (Electroencephalography) measures the brain's electrical activity. This test captures electrical signals from the brain and displays them on a graph, aiding in the diagnosis of brain disorders such as epilepsy and sleep issues. EEG is painless and safe, making it a valuable tool for understanding brain function.
